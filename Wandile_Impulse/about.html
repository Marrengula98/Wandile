About Impul$e

Wandile Nkabinde stage name Impul$e
25 years old
Born and Bred in Umlazi Z section
Start making music in early 2019 with Debut EP Fatal of 5 tracks with his best friend and producer Nerdistic Touch
In the same year he got to open in a small show in Durban for Hip Hip Sensation Frank Casino
He makes Hip Hop and RnB music
In 2020 he released a small EP called Mi Corazon ( My Heart ) with just 3 tracks
With his highest streamed song on there being Thorn In A Rose ( 7k streams )
In 2021 he later released his much anticipated EP ,NOT EVERYTHING THAT GLITTERS IS GOLD , on all platforms featuring local artists and friends.
Following the success of the EP , he hosted a show in 2022 on the 11 of March to launch the mixtape at Well known club origin in the Glenwood region.
His latest set of songs come from a beautiful EP titled Subdued in Love, which he later hosted a show for at the harbour in Durban
